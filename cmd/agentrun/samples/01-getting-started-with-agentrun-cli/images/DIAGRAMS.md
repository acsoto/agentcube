## Recommended Colors

- **Developer Actions**: Blue (#3498db)
- **CLI Commands**: Green (#2ecc71)
- **Agent Components**: Orange (#e67e22)
- **External Services**: Purple (#9b59b6)
- **Data Flow**: Gray arrows (#95a5a6)
- **Success States**: Green (#27ae60)
- **Error States**: Red (#e74c3c)

## Tools for Creating Diagrams

- **draw.io** (free, web-based)
- **Lucidchart** (collaborative)
- **Excalidraw** (simple, hand-drawn style)
- **PlantUML** (text-based)
- **Mermaid** (markdown-based)

## Diagram Specifications

- **Format**: PNG
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or higher
- **Background**: White or transparent
- **Font**: Sans-serif, minimum 14pt for readability
- **File naming**: lowercase with underscores (e.g., `workflow.png`)
